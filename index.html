WATER RESOURCES

With a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science and a Master's degree in Civil Engineering for Water Resources and Hydrology, I am passionate about finding solutions to water and ecology issues from local to global scales. Whether solutions come in the form of hydrologic models, geospatial maps, database applications, field sampling, or infographics, I am eager to contribute my knowledge and skills. I love anything to do with water, including volunteering for the Water Youth Network, hiking by an alpine creek or Northwest river, and smelling the petrichor from a fresh rain.


ENVIRONMENTAL DATA SCIENCE

Playing with data greatly excites me! Whether the data is about streamflow, weather patterns, social demographics, you name it, I would love to explore its hidden trends and secrets. I am drawn to data science because it is logical, mathematical, creative, artistic, visual, and enables communication of complex topics. I welcome my nerdy side, and am passionate about learning new coding languages while constantly developing my skills as a data scientist. It is quite common for me to giggle with excitement when I discover a new pattern or statistic in data.

CREATIVE DESIGN

I love to express my creative side, and constantly strive to combine art with environmental science. I believe science communiciation will continue to play a vital role in water and environmental issues. I am dedicated to expressing complex scientific concepts through visualizations, in fact it has always been my dream! You can find me sighing from the beauty of Planet Earth or Blue Planet episodes, painting, developing graphic design images, taking photographs, and writing stories.
