WATER RESOURCES

With a Bachelor's degree in Environmental Science and a Master's degree in Civil Engineering for Water Resources and Hydrology, I am passionate about water, nature, ecology, and math. I particularly enjoy solving challenging mathematical problems in relation to water resources. Whether solutions come in the form of hydrologic models, geospatial maps, database applications, field sampling, or infographics, I am eager to contribute my knowledge and skills. I love anything to do with water, including volunteering for the Water Youth Network, hiking by an alpine creek or Northwest river, and smelling the petrichor from a fresh rain.

ENVIRONMENTAL DATA SCIENCE

I love to expore the hidden trends and secrets in data. Whether the data is about streamflow, weather patterns, social demographics, you name it, I am excited to play with it! I am drawn to data science because it is logical, mathematical, creative, artistic, visual, and enables communication of complex topics. I welcome my nerdy side, and am passionate about learning new coding languages while developing my skills as a data scientist. I often giggle with excitement when I discover a new pattern or statistic in data.
